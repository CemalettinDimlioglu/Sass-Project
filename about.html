BIO

Vikings is a Canadian-Irish co-production television series in the "Vikings" historical drama genre.[3] The series, written and produced by Michael Hirst, is broadcast on the History channel.[2] It started broadcasting on March 3, 2013 in the USA and Canada. Filmed in Ireland, Vikings is inspired by the life and stories of Viking Ragnar Lodbrok. Ragnar Lodbrok has become the hero of the most well-known Norse mythology and the scourge of infamous France and England. Ragnar, portrayed as a Viking farmer, made his first raids on England with his brother Rollo and his wife, the warrior Lagertha. On April 5, 2013, History channel announced that the second season of Vikings, which started on February 27, 2014[4][5] will have ten episodes.[6] On March 25, 2014, History channel announced that the third season of Vikings will have ten episodes.[7]
